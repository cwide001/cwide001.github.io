Adventure Guide


Rainbow Valley $299/ Person

Have you ever gone rafting before? If not, this is one of our best adventures for the beginners. It is so much fun, you're whole family, no matter the age can go! It will involve an over night stay at one of the famous sand shorelines that accompanies the river. Pick one of our guides to help you navigate the rapids and back home! Please check out our reservation page for more information!


Salmon Falls $500/ Person

Want a little more action? This adventure let's you put your skills to the test. Pick one of our famous tour guides to help you learn how to go off a waterfall. Yes, you read that right. You will be literally falling of a waterfall to get to the end of this crazy adventure. If you want more information on this intense adventure, please refer to our reservations page.


Devil's road $650/ Person

Feeling a little dangerous? This is a beast of an adventure. It is not named after the Devil himself for no reason. This trip will test your limits and skills. If you have not rafted before, you need to think again before booking this trip. You must be 21 years of age or older. At the end of this, if you survive, we will be celebrating with some Devil's BBQ, which is included in the price. For more information, please refer to the reservations page.
