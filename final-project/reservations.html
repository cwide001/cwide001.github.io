Rainbow Valley $299

This river is for the novice. Those who have never done white water rafting will love this 3 day experience. It is not a gentle ride like some may think, but a very exciting and fast ride. You will not leave dry. Make sure you pack a poncho! This includes a two-day adventure that will include food and traveling to the drop location. Once dropped off, everything will be done by foot. Bring warm clothing and sleeping gear. Tents will be provided. Dont think you will leave dry, so pack extra clothes!


Salmon Falls $500

This river is for the intermediate rafter. If you feel confident enough to try going off a waterfall, this river is for you. There is a HUGE drop on the river that allows those will some experience to taste something new. It will be an exciting time with Katie. She will be your tour guide on this fun adventure. You will fall in love with this river adventure. This includes a three-day adventure that will include food and traveling to the drop location. Once dropped off, everything will be done by foot. Bring warm clothing and sleeping gear. Tents will be provided. Dont think you will leave dry, so pack extra clothes!


Devil's road $650

This is a river for those with the most experience. This river is Tom's baby. Tom is one of our most trained and experienced tour guides. He will be the host for those adventurous enough to ride Devil's road. If you are just starting out, you will not be allowed to try this until after you sign a waiver. This includes a four-day adventure that will include food and traveling to the drop location. Once dropped off, everything will be done by foot. Bring warm clothing and sleeping gear. Tents will be provided as this will be a thrill of a ride through Devil's road.
