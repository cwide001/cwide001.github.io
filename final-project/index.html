Welcome to Salmon River Adventures!

Riggins Idaho is known for some of the best white water rafting adventures! Come and experience what true happiness is like. If you want to bring a group or come by yourself, we have adventures for everyone. We have tour guides that will take you on adventures based on your level of experience. If you are a beginner, we have Tom who will lead you. If you have more experience, we have others who will be excited to help you find your way down the Salmon River. Don't be afraid, you will never have another experience like this one! Check out our adventure guide and reservation page for more details!
