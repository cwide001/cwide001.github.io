We are Corona Virus Free!

by Corey Wideen

We are struggling to find happiness in these dark times. You do not have to worry! Here in Fish Haven, we are Corona Virus free and provide a healthy way of living. We have toilet paper in stock and water! No need to panic. We are a great place to live and have many things to do!

Share with your friends this amazing news. This truly is God's Country. We have rolling hills and beautiful lakes. The lake is open year around and we provide some of the best catches. We have had a record year with a salmon that was recorded with a weight of 22 pounds! Please come visit, the weather here is amazing!
