Description of Audience

The target audience are people who are interested in learning about the weather in their area and how it will affect them. Anyone is welcome to learn this information and will be accesible for all!

First Person

The first person is a 22 year old named Jeff. He works on his grandpa's property fixing things and feeding animals. He wants to know when he can take the animals out of the barn to clean it. He checks the website for weather information in his area.

2nd person

The second Person is a 35 year old mother who has 5 children and a husband. They are planning a trip to Soda Springs and wants to know when they should go. She is planning to attend the water park with her family and wants to go on the hottest day of the week. She checks the website to see what the 10-day forecast is going to be.
